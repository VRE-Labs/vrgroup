    • Staff receives a formal warning from leadership.
    • A 30-day Improvement Plan is created with specific goals & behavioral expectations.
    • Staff must check in regularly with a mentor/mod lead for progress updates.

  • Used for: Continuous issues, a single major offense, or failure to improve in Tier 2.
  • Examples: Clear favoritism, passive-aggressive behavior, dismissing community concerns.
  • Action Taken:
    • Staff is placed on probation for 60 days with restricted permissions.
    • They must undergo additional training & supervision by leadership.
    • A final behavior assessment is conducted before probation ends.
    • If significant improvement is shown, they are reinstated with full permissions.
    • If no improvement is shown, they are moved to Tier 4.

    • A permanent or temporary ban may be considered, depending on severity.
    • Community & remaining staff are informed of the change in leadership (without unnecessary drama).
