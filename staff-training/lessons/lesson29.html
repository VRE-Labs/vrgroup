Lesson 29: A.C.T. Campaign

Active Community Theatrics - Recurring themed events to inspire creativity and engagement.

1. 🎭 What is the A.C.T. Campaign?

The A.C.T. Campaign is a series of recurring themed events or community spotlights designed to:

  • Inspire Creativity: Encourage artistic and creative expression.
  • Build Engagement: Keep the community active between major events.
  • Foster Connections: Bring members together through shared interests and talents.

2. 🛠️ How It Works

  • Themes: Each campaign has a unique theme, such as writing prompts, avatar showcases, or talent nights.
  • Frequency: Events are held regularly to maintain momentum and interest.
  • Participation: Open to all members, with opportunities to showcase their work or talents.
  • Spotlights: Highlight outstanding contributions to celebrate creativity and effort.

3. 🎧 Staff Responsibilities

  • Plan and announce themes in advance to build anticipation.
  • Facilitate events by providing clear instructions and guidelines.
  • Encourage participation and provide constructive feedback.
  • Highlight and celebrate contributions to motivate others.

4. 📋 A.C.T. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Choose themes that appeal to a wide range of interests.
  • Promote inclusivity and encourage all skill levels to participate.
  • Provide a platform for members to share their work.

❌ Don’t:

  • Overcomplicate the themes or guidelines.
  • Criticize contributions harshly or discourage participation.
  • Let the campaign lose momentum by skipping scheduled events.

5. 📎 Tracking Impact

After each campaign, staff should note:

  • Number of participants and types of contributions.
  • Feedback from attendees about the theme and event structure.
  • Any standout moments or highlights to share with the community.

This helps refine future campaigns and ensures they remain engaging and impactful.
