Lesson 28: S.P.A.R.K. Sessions

Staff Presence & Activity to Reinforce Kinship - Casual pop-up VC hangouts to boost morale and engagement.

1. 🌟 What are S.P.A.R.K. Sessions?

S.P.A.R.K. Sessions are short, casual VC hangouts hosted by staff to:

  • Boost Morale: Create a fun and relaxed atmosphere for the community.
  • Reinforce Presence: Remind members that staff are approachable and engaged.
  • Foster Kinship: Strengthen bonds between staff and the community.

2. 🛠️ How It Works

  • Pop-Up Format: Sessions are informal and can be scheduled spontaneously.
  • Short Duration: Typically 30 minutes to an hour.
  • Casual Themes: Examples include meme sharing, "what are you up to?" check-ins, or "bring your pet to VC" hangouts.
  • Inclusive: Open to all community members to join and participate.

3. 🎧 Staff Responsibilities

  • Be welcoming and inclusive to all participants.
  • Encourage lighthearted and positive interactions.
  • Facilitate the session by introducing the theme or activity.
  • Ensure the environment remains respectful and fun.

4. 📋 S.P.A.R.K. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Keep the tone light and engaging.
  • Encourage participation from everyone.
  • Adapt to the vibe of the group and keep things flowing.

❌ Don’t:

  • Let the session become overly formal or structured.
  • Exclude or ignore quieter participants.
  • Allow negativity or disruptive behavior to persist.

5. 📎 Tracking Impact

After each session, staff should note:

  • Number of participants and overall engagement level.
  • Feedback or suggestions from attendees.
  • Any notable moments or highlights.

This helps refine future sessions and ensures they remain impactful.
