Lesson 26: R.E.A.C.H. - A Re-engagement Initiative

Reconnect, Engage, Appreciate, Check-in, Help inactive members rejoin the community.

1. 🌟 What is R.E.A.C.H.?

R.E.A.C.H. is a structured initiative to re-engage inactive members by:

  • Reconnect: Reach out to members who have been inactive for a while.
  • Engage: Invite them to participate in events or discussions.
  • Appreciate: Show gratitude for their past contributions.
  • Check-in: Ask about their well-being and any challenges they face.
  • Help: Offer assistance to make their return seamless.

2. 🛠️ Core Steps

  • Identify: Use activity logs to find members who have been inactive for over a month.
  • Personalize: Craft personalized messages that show genuine interest in their return.
  • Invite: Share upcoming events or initiatives that might interest them.
  • Follow-up: Check back after a week to see if they need further assistance.

3. 🎧 Communication Tips

  • Be friendly and non-intrusive.
  • Use positive language to make them feel valued.
  • Respect their decision if they choose not to re-engage.

4. 📋 R.E.A.C.H. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Send personalized messages.
  • Highlight community updates and improvements.
  • Offer support and guidance for rejoining.

❌ Don’t:

  • Spam members with repeated messages.
  • Pressure them to return immediately.
  • Ignore their feedback or concerns.

5. 📎 Tracking Progress

Keep a record of:

  • Members contacted and their responses.
  • Events they attended after re-engaging.
  • Feedback received during the process.

This helps refine the initiative and measure its success.
