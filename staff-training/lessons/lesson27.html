Lesson 27: S.P.R.O.U.T. Program

Support, Participation, Recognition, Outreach, Uplift, Trust - A mentorship program for newer members.

1. 🌱 What is the S.P.R.O.U.T. Program?

The S.P.R.O.U.T. Program is VRE’s mentorship initiative designed to:

  • Support: Provide guidance to newer members.
  • Participation: Encourage active involvement in the community.
  • Recognition: Celebrate achievements and contributions.
  • Outreach: Foster connections with trusted regulars.
  • Uplift: Build confidence and a sense of belonging.
  • Trust: Create a safe space for questions and concerns.

2. 🛠️ How It Works

  • Pairing: New members are paired with trusted, welcoming regulars (mentors).
  • Guidance: Mentors provide advice, answer questions, and help navigate the community.
  • Check-ins: Regular check-ins to ensure the new member feels supported.
  • Events: Encourage participation in events and activities to build connections.

3. 🎧 Mentor Responsibilities

  • Be approachable and responsive to questions.
  • Encourage participation in community events and discussions.
  • Provide constructive feedback and celebrate progress.
  • Act as a role model by demonstrating VRE’s values.

4. 📋 S.P.R.O.U.T. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Be patient and understanding with new members.
  • Offer encouragement and positive reinforcement.
  • Share resources and tips to help them succeed.

❌ Don’t:

  • Overwhelm new members with too much information at once.
  • Ignore their questions or concerns.
  • Pressure them to participate in activities they’re uncomfortable with.

5. 📎 Tracking Progress

Mentors should keep track of:

  • Interactions and check-ins with their mentees.
  • Events or activities their mentees participate in.
  • Feedback from mentees about their experience.

This helps ensure the program is effective and provides valuable insights for improvement.
