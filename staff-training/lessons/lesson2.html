Lesson 2: How to Correct Behavior Proactively

Learn how to address behavioral issues early to maintain a positive community environment.

Introduction

Proactive Behavioral Correction (PBC) is a strategy designed to address behavioral issues before they escalate. Instead of waiting for someone to break the rules, we step in early to guide them in the right direction. This helps keep VREvents fun, respectful, and fair for everyone while avoiding unnecessary punishments.
