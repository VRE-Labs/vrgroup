Lesson 38: V.O.I.C.E. Platform

Visibility, Openness, Inclusion, Community, Expression - A speaker series or VC event for sharing knowledge and stories.

1. 📢 What is the V.O.I.C.E. Platform?

The V.O.I.C.E. Platform is a speaker series or VC event where members or staff can:

  • Share Knowledge: Present on topics they’re passionate about.
  • Tell Stories: Share personal experiences or creative narratives.
  • Host Discussions: Facilitate open forums or debates on relevant topics.
  • Express Themselves: Showcase their unique perspectives and ideas.

Think “TEDTalks for VRE,” but way more relatable and chaotic.

2. 🛠️ How It Works

  • Event Scheduling: Regularly scheduled sessions with a mix of pre-planned and open-mic slots.
  • Speaker Sign-Ups: Members or staff can sign up to present or host a discussion.
  • Moderation: A moderator ensures the event stays on track and respectful.
  • Recording & Sharing: Sessions can be recorded and shared for those who couldn’t attend live.

3. 🎧 Staff Responsibilities

  • Promote the platform and encourage participation from the community.
  • Assist speakers with preparation and technical setup if needed.
  • Moderate sessions to ensure a positive and inclusive environment.
  • Collect feedback to improve future events.

4. 📋 V.O.I.C.E. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Encourage diverse topics and perspectives.
  • Foster an open and respectful atmosphere.
  • Provide constructive feedback to speakers.

❌ Don’t:

  • Allow discussions to become hostile or disrespectful.
  • Over-schedule events, leading to burnout or low attendance.
  • Ignore feedback from participants or speakers.

5. 📎 Tracking Impact

To measure the success of the V.O.I.C.E. Platform, track:

  • Number of participants and speakers per session.
  • Engagement metrics, such as questions asked or feedback provided.
  • Community sentiment and suggestions for improvement.

This ensures the platform remains engaging and valuable for the community.
