Lesson 31: G.E.M.S. Recognition

Giving Exceptional Members Spotlights - Highlighting uplifting, active, or all-around awesome members.

1. 💎 What is G.E.M.S. Recognition?

The G.E.M.S. Recognition program is designed to:

  • Celebrate: Highlight members who are uplifting, active, or exceptional in the community.
  • Encourage: Motivate others to contribute positively by showcasing role models.
  • Reward: Provide small perks, shout-outs, or special Discord badges/titles.

2. 🛠️ How It Works

  • Peer Nominations: Members can nominate others who have made a positive impact.
  • Staff Review: Staff reviews nominations to ensure fairness and alignment with community values.
  • Frequency: Recognitions are announced monthly or bi-weekly.
  • Rewards: Shout-outs, small perks, or a special Discord badge/title are given to recognized members.

3. 🎧 Staff Responsibilities

  • Encourage members to submit nominations and explain why they’re nominating someone.
  • Review nominations objectively and ensure a diverse range of members are recognized.
  • Announce recognitions in a fun and engaging way to celebrate the recipients.
  • Coordinate rewards, such as assigning badges or titles, and ensure they are delivered promptly.

4. 📋 G.E.M.S. Do’s and Don’ts

✅ Do:

  • Encourage nominations from all community members.
  • Highlight a variety of contributions, from activity to kindness to creativity.
  • Ensure the recognition process is transparent and inclusive.

❌ Don’t:

  • Favor the same members repeatedly without considering others.
  • Ignore nominations without providing a reason.
  • Delay announcements or rewards, which can reduce the program’s impact.

5. 📎 Tracking Impact

To measure the success of the G.E.M.S. Recognition program, track:

  • Number of nominations submitted each cycle.
  • Feedback from the community about the program.
  • Engagement levels of recognized members after receiving recognition.

This ensures the program remains meaningful and continues to inspire positive contributions.
