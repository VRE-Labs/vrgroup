II. Moderation Tools in VRChat

VRChat offers its own built-in moderation tools designed to ensure users can manage instances and groups effectively. These tools allow you to take quick action when disruptive behavior arises.

VRChat Moderation Tools

  • Force Mute: If a user is causing disruption through voice chat, you can forcefully mute them. This ensures that they cannot continue to disturb others, and it can be a first step in addressing inappropriate behavior.
  • Warnings: VRChat has a built-in Warning button. While we encourage verbal warnings as a first resort, it may become necessary to escalate to a physical warning.
  • Kick from Instance: If a user is being disruptive but their actions are not severe enough to warrant a ban, you can kick them from the instance. This removes them temporarily and prevents further disruption within that specific session.
  • Ban from Group: If a user continues to cause problems and is not responding to warnings or kicks, you can ban them from the group or instance. This is a more serious action and should be used in cases of repeated violations or severe disruption.

When to Use VRChat Moderation Tools

  • Voice Disturbance: If a user is repeatedly interrupting or speaking inappropriately, use the Force Mute feature to stop the disruption. This is especially important in VRChat where voice communication is key to the experience.
  • Minor Disruptions: For less severe disturbances, such as inappropriate behavior or actions that donâ€™t violate community guidelines directly, a kick from the instance can be effective. This gives the user a chance to adjust their behavior without being permanently banned.
  • Severe Violations: If a userâ€™s actions are damaging to the communityâ€”such as harassment, hate speech, or serious rule-breakingâ€”it may be necessary to ban them from the group. Bans should be considered as a last resort after attempting less severe actions, like warnings and kicks.

III. Best Practices for Moderation

  • Be Fair and Consistent: Moderators should always apply the rules consistently. Ensure that the same guidelines are followed for all users, regardless of their status or relationships with staff.
  • Use Escalating Measures: Start with a warning or a less severe action (such as a mute or kick), and escalate the severity of the punishment only if the behavior continues.
  • Communicate with Users: Whenever possible, communicate with users before taking action. Let them know why their behavior is inappropriate, and give them a chance to correct it.
  • Keep Records: In Discord, Volt logs all actions taken by moderators, which is crucial for transparency and accountability. In VRChat, while actions like kicks and bans may be recorded, it's also helpful to document any user interactions for future reference in case of disputes.
  • Avoid Abuse of Power: Moderators should avoid using their power for personal gain or to settle personal conflicts. Always use moderation tools to protect the community and ensure a positive experience for everyone.

Conclusion

Both Discord and VRChat provide robust moderation tools to help maintain a safe and enjoyable experience for all users. By using Volt, our custom Discord bot, along with the built-in tools provided by VRChat, you can effectively manage your community and handle disruptive behavior. Remember to always apply moderation actions fairly, escalate when necessary, and communicate with users to ensure a positive environment.
