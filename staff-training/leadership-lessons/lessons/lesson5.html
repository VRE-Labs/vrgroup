Event roles are temporary assignments based on staff strengths, availability, and comfort level. Rotating these roles ensures fairness, prevents burnout, and helps staff develop new skills.

Key Points:

  • Roles are not tied to permanent ranks.
  • Rotation builds leadership depth and prevents role hoarding.
