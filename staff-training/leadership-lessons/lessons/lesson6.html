🌟 Understanding the H.M.U Ratio

“Building Sustainable, Balanced Event Staffing for VREvents.”

🧠 Section 1: What is the H.M.U Ratio?

The H.M.U Ratio refers to the proportion of Hosts, Moderators, and Users assigned to an event. It ensures that leadership has the support it needs while preventing overburdening any one person or role.

Key Points:

  • Hosts manage event creation, promotion, and facilitation.
  • Moderators ensure community guidelines are followed and resolve conflicts.
  • Users are the participants who engage in the event.

Question: What does the H.M.U Ratio stand for?

⚙️ Section 2: Staffing Plan Guidelines

To maintain a balanced event, follow these guidelines:

  • Host to User Ratio: 1 Host for every 20-30 Users.
  • Mod to User Ratio: 1 Moderator for every 15-20 Users.
  • User Groups: Scale staff proportionally as user participation increases.

🌀 Section 3: Preventing Burnout

Preventing burnout is essential for sustainable staffing:

  • Delegate Roles: Assign tasks based on strengths and interests.
  • Breaks and Rotation: Rotate roles and provide breaks during events.
  • Use Support Staff: Delegate smaller tasks to lighten the load.
