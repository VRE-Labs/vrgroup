What is a F.L.A.R.E. Report?

F.L.A.R.E. Reports are post-event evaluations designed to capture key insights and feedback from events hosted by Virtual Reality Entertainment (VRE). These reports help us reflect on what went well, identify areas for improvement, and ensure continuous growth in our event planning and execution.

How F.L.A.R.E. Reports Work

Each report is completed by the Lead Host, with contributions from moderators and leadership. The report is structured into five key sections:

  • Feedback: Captures the overall vibe, attendee feedback, and unexpected occurrences.
  • Learnings: Highlights what went well, standout moments, and positive feedback.
  • Attendance: Tracks attendee numbers, peak times, and engagement levels.
  • Reflection: Identifies areas for improvement and challenges faced.
  • Enhancements: Suggests changes and additional support needed for future events.
