🔎 Overall Moderation Performance Assessment

📌 Strengths Observed:

  • ✅ Swift Action Against Rule Violations: All reported disruptive users were immediately removed, ensuring the event remained a safe and inclusive space. No incidents escalated beyond verbal altercations.
  • ✅ Adherence to Zero-Tolerance Policy: Moderators upheld strict enforcement against harassment, hate speech, and potential threats, reinforcing community standards.
  • ✅ Effective Use of Instance Kicks & Group Bans: Moderation actions were proportionate to the offenses. The one group ban issued was in response to a potential high-risk user, demonstrating proactive risk mitigation.

⚠️ Areas for Improvement:

  • ❌ Insufficient Moderator Presence: The event experienced a period where only one moderator was present due to another mod crashing. While no severe disruptions occurred, this created a coverage gap that could have led to a safety risk.
    • 🔹 Solution: Ensure adherence to the 1:30 Moderator-to-User ratio by assigning backup moderators and emphasizing redundancy in case of unexpected disconnects.
  • ❌ Unconfirmed Report Handling: While the immediate ban of RockySi709 was a necessary precaution due to minors being present, the lack of verification raises concerns about due diligence.
    • 🔹 Solution: Implement a structured review process for handling high-risk reports, possibly requiring more verification before group bans. A temporary instance kick followed by rapid escalation to leads may be a better approach.

📊 Final Rating

Moderation Performance: 7.5/10

🔹 What Went Well: Quick, decisive actions prevented disruptions. Moderators enforced policies effectively.

🔹 What Needs Work: Better backup coverage, improved handling of serious but unverified reports.

🛠️ Action Plan Moving Forward

  1. 1️⃣ Reinforce 1:30 Mod-to-User Ratio Compliance: Ensure there are at least two active moderators at all times, with an on-call backup.
  2. 2️⃣ Enhance Crash Contingency Plans: If a moderator crashes, a backup must immediately step in.
  3. 3️⃣ Refine Protocol for Unconfirmed Reports: Consider temporary removals before permanent bans, except in cases of imminent risk.
  4. 4️⃣ Review & Debrief: Gather moderator feedback and adjust protocols for future events.
